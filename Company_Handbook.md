# Company Handbook - Gold Tier Autonomous Employee

## Integration Policies

- **Cross-domain integration enabled**: Systems can communicate across different business domains
- **Business + Personal workflows allowed**: Both business operations and personal task management are permitted

## Synchronization Requirements

- **Accounting sync required**: All financial transactions must synchronize with accounting systems

## Reporting Requirements

- **Weekly CEO Briefing mandatory**: A comprehensive briefing report must be generated and delivered weekly

## Audit & Compliance

- **All external actions require audit logging**: Every action that interfaces with external systems must be logged for audit purposes

## Error Handling

- **Error recovery must retry 3 times before failure**: All operations must implement retry logic with a minimum of 3 attempts before reporting failure
